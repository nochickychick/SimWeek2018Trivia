Answers and rationales for the Sim Week 2018 Trivia Quiz

Question Six: What year did FNU begin using simulation in the education of its students?


Possible answers:

  1. 1900
  2. 1939
  3. 1985
  4. 2010

correct answer: B. 1939

Rationale:

Even at the beginning of its programming, simulation techniques were used to help students understand how to perform procedures and care for patients. That concept is not new, however, what is new is the technologically advanced method in which we are able to do this.
