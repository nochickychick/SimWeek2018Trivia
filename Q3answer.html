Question Three: Constructivist learning theory uses all of the following EXCEPT:


Possible answers:

  • A. Self Reflection
  • B. Problem-solving
  • C. Critical Reflection
  • D. Shaping by others

correct answer: D. Shaping by others

Rationale:

Constructivism is a learner-centered, "active" learning theory. The learner constructs knowledge by building on previous experiences. In constructivism, "doing" becomes learning. The goals of constructivism include problem solving, critical thinking, and reflective use of information. In constructivism, self-awareness and reflection play a large part in constructing knowledge. Faculty's role in the constructivist approach is that of coach and facilitator, guiding the learner as he/she builds new knowledge.
