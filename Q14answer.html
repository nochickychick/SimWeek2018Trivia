Question Fourteen: There is a difference between assessment and evaluation.


Possible answers:

  • True
  • False

correct answer: True

Rationale:

This is another one of those 'word' questions :) Technically, there is a difference between assessment and evaluation. However, like many educational terms, these are often used interchangably. In general terms, assessment is formative. It is a measurement or observation we make with the intent or focus on improvement. Evaluation is summative. It often involves measuring performance against a standard. Neither is more important than the other, they are just different.
