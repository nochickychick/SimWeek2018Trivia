Question Sixteen: When you run into problems with developing assessment methods, where should you look first for solutions?


Possible answers:

  1. Other courses
  2. Your objectives
  3. Prior assessment score trends
  4. Online

correct answer: B. Your objectives

Rationale:

All of the above can be helpful sources of information when having issues with assessments, however, the best place to start is at the root of the assessment itself--your objectives. One of the most common errors we come across is objectives that are not congruent with the methods used to assess them. This this is especially common when objectives aren't determined from the begininng of the design process. Misalignment can result in frustration on the part of both the student and the instructor, and can have a negative effect on course design, student learning outcomes, and course evaluation.
